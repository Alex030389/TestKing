  • 1. Which different Testking products are available?

    There are four different Testking products available for your certification and exam training:

    • Questions and Answers (Q&A)

      Questions and Answers product should be used as preparation before the exam and comes with a pass guaranteed. It includes questions and correct answers with explanations (where available) and covers exactly the same topics as required to pass Exam.

    • Study Guides

      Study Guides provide theoretical background and the practical know-how regarding the subject. You download the Study Guide document in PDF format and use it on your local PC.

    • Simulation Labs

      Simulation Labs are available online for many of our CompTIA, Microsoft, VMware and other exams. These are lab scenarios on how to solve the problems as well as detailed tutorial and lab solutions so that people can practice on their own equipment. They give overview of real hardware/software configurations so that you become familiar with the Testing Environment.

    • Audio Exams

      Audio Exams are easy to download and take with you MP3 files. Audio Exam allows you to make any time, productive time. Recommended for people with busy schedules.

  • 2. How will I access Questions & Answers Product?

    Testking Question & Answer products are formulated in form of Interactive Testing Engine. This is downloaded, installed, and used on your local PC. It simulates the real exam environment and enables you to take a Virtual Exam (test yourself with exam questions with a time limit), Practice exam (review exam questions one by one, see correct answers and explanations). You can view your exam history, change order of questions and answers, search for questions and answers, add notes and many other options.

  • 3. How long is my product Valid?

    Product is valid for 90 days from the date of purchase. After this Duration the Expired product will no longer be accessible.

  • 4. What format is used for Study Guides?

    Study Guides can be access as PDFs and downloaded on computer.

  • 5. I'd like to try before I buy. Can I try the Demo for Online Testing Engine for Free?

    Yes, we have Demos available for several Testing Engines available in our samples page. Currently we provide only samples of popular exams. Please, e-mail feedback@testking.com and state which sample you would like to receive.

  • 6. What does your Q&A with explanations entail?

    Our Question & Answer with explanations contain simulated exam questions with the most accurate answers. Explanations are provided for all the options so it helps you to understand why a specific Answers option is correct or incorrect. More information about available Q&A can be found on our products page.

  • 7. Do all Testking's Questions & Answer include explanations?

    No. Our goal is to provide explanations to our entire set of products but currently we are offering this feature for only the Exams that have high demand in Certification Market.

  • 8. How can I detect if a product has explanations or not before I make the purchase?

    The products page for each Exam or Certification will specifically say if the product is Questions and Answers, or Questions and Answers with Explanations.

  • 9. Can I pass an exam with Testking Question & Answers only?

    Yes, it is possible. However, we recommend that you use one extra source, such as the Testking Study Guide, Lab preparation or Audio Exam to provide the theoretical background and the practical hands-on experience.

  • 10. What exactly is in your Study Guides?

    Study Guides are designed to ensure that you have the required knowledge to pass the respective exam at first attempt. They consist of detailed concepts that are tested in the exam as well as a lab sections where you can learn the practical implementation of concepts. More information about available study guides can be found on our products page.

  • 11. What are the system requirements to use the Testking products?

    At the moment there are four requirements:

    - You need a Windows operating system
    - You need have the permissions to install a program in Windows
    - Your computer must be able to access the Internet
    - You need to install the Java Runtime Environment (JRE)

    We are planning to provide our products for Mac and Linux operating systems in the future, but at the moment Windows is a requirement
